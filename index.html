Jason Mayden

Jason Mayden is a pioneering designer and entrepreneur recognized for his groundbreaking work in footwear design and technology. Joining Nike at just 22, he became one of the youngest designers in the company's history. During his tenure, he contributed to iconic projects like the Air Jordan line, leaving an indelible mark on sneaker culture. Beyond footwear, Mayden co-founded Super Heroic, a company focused on creating innovative footwear and apparel for children, blending style with functionality to promote active play. His designs often integrate cutting-edge technology, such as augmented reality, to encourage physical activity and creativity in kids. Passionate about diversity and inclusion, Mayden advocates for underrepresented communities in tech and design, using his platform to mentor and inspire the next generation of creators. He is a vocal proponent of design education, emphasizing its importance in fostering creativity and problem-solving skills. Mayden's work extends beyond commercial success; he is dedicated to social impact, addressing issues like access to quality footwear and designing inclusively for people with disabilities. His achievements have garnered recognition, including features in esteemed publications and prestigious awards, solidifying his legacy as a visionary leader in design and entrepreneurship.
